Pong

A more modern take on the arcade classic of the 1970's.
"W" and "S" keys control the left pong. "UP" and "DOWN" arrow keys control the right pong. Use your pong to rebound the moving ball and prevent it from passing your side of the screen. Scores are kept at the top of the game screen.

Platformer

A 2D horizontal platformer designed by students in the JavaScript for Beginners course.
Use the "W", "A" and "D" keys to control the character's jump, left and right movement.
